Hi, I'm Anjali Singh

I read a lot, then journal, but lately I'm building.

Currently learning to code, one entry at a time — reading the logic to make this "logic journaling" work.

I've always found it easier to understand myself through words — books first, then journaling my own thoughts and realties. Writing has always been how I process things and my life. Lately, I've started learning to code, and I realized this could be another way to write: instead of pages, I'm building a page. This site is where that learning lives.

I know learning can be hard but i have always loved the feeling which comes with being smart enough to learn it all.Nowdays, I can feel stuck but if you would not know the feeling of being stuck you can never crave the freedom.So yeah here is to always learning>>>.
